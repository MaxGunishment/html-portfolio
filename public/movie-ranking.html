The Best Films According to Liam

My favourite three films of all time

1. One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest

A terrifying, but bittersweet film.

2. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

An emotional tale about love, pain, and loss.

3. Braindead

The original and best Zom-Com from Peter Jackson, who went on to direct
The Lord of the Rings trilogy.
